I am actively seeking collaborators across population genomics, pharmacogenomics, immunogenetics, molecular diagnostics, and public health genomics. If any of the areas below align with your work, I would welcome a conversation.

---

## What I Am Looking For

### Co-write grants
I am preparing applications to several funding bodies and welcome co-applicants with complementary expertise, particularly in bioinformatics, statistical genetics, clinical genomics, health economics, and ELSI. If you have a grant idea that needs an East African genomics partner, reach out.

### Co-author papers
I have datasets, analyses, and manuscripts in preparation across population genomics, pharmacogenomics, HLA diversity, and antimicrobial resistance. I welcome collaborators who bring analytical depth, clinical context, or complementary population data.

### Co-supervise students
I supervise MSc and PhD students at Meru University of Science and Technology. I welcome co-supervision arrangements with faculty at other institutions, particularly for students working on genomics, bioinformatics, immunogenetics, or pharmacogenomics in African populations.

### Host visiting researchers
Our laboratory in Nairobi is available for visiting researchers who need access to genomics infrastructure, East African samples, or a base for fieldwork in the region.
