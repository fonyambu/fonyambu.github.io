## Ongoing Research Projects

### Kenya Reference Genome Initiative
**Role:** Principal Investigator | **Period:** 2024-present

A population-scale genomic study generating 30x whole genome sequencing data from 1,000 Kenyan adults across all 47 counties and 46 ethnolinguistic groups. Pilot data from 15 genomes has identified over 18 million variants, 9.23% novel to gnomAD v4.1, with 100% of participants carrying clinically actionable pharmacogenomic variants. Part of the broader East Africa Genome Reference Initiative led by [CMB Genomics](https://www.cmbgenomics.org).

---

### The Africa Cancer Atlas
**Role:** Investigator | **Period:** 2022-present

Multi-country cancer genomics programme characterising the molecular landscape of cancers across African populations. Collaborative project involving clinical sites, pathologists, and genomics centres across the continent.

---

### East African HLA and Immunogenetics Atlas
**Role:** Co-Investigator | **Period:** 2024-present

Sequencing-based HLA typing (Class I and Class II) and KIR diversity analysis across East African populations. Building allele frequency and haplotype resources for vaccine design, transplant immunology, and drug hypersensitivity.

---

### HPV Screening in Women Living with HIV
**Role:** Co-Investigator | **Period:** 2022-present

Molecular characterisation of high-risk HPV genotype prevalence and distribution among women living with HIV in Kenya. Informing targeted vaccination and screening strategies. Active sites in Meru and Kilifi counties.

---

### Validation of Point-of-Care Device for STI Testing in Kenya
**Role:** Investigator | **Period:** Ongoing

Evaluating a point-of-care diagnostic device for sexually transmitted infection testing in Kenyan clinical settings. Assessing sensitivity, specificity, and operational feasibility in resource-limited environments.

---

### Vaginal Microbiome in Heavy Menstrual Bleeding
**Role:** Co-Investigator | **Period:** Ongoing

Characterising the vaginal microbiome in women presenting with heavy menstrual bleeding. Exploring microbial community composition and its association with clinical outcomes.

---

### Self-Sampling Approaches for STI Screening
**Role:** Co-Investigator | **Period:** Ongoing

Evaluating self-sampling methods for sexually transmitted infection screening to improve access and uptake in underserved populations. Assessing acceptability, feasibility, and diagnostic performance.

---

### Pharmacogenomics of East African Populations
**Role:** Principal Investigator | **Period:** 2024-present

Characterising pharmacogenomic variant frequencies across East African ethnolinguistic groups. Star allele calling and metabolizer phenotype distributions for CYP2D6, CYP2C19, CYP3A5, DPYD, TPMT, UGT1A1, and SLCO1B1. Pilot data shows a mean of 4.6 actionable genes per individual.

---

### Antimicrobial Resistance in Livestock-Wildlife Interface
**Role:** Co-Investigator | **Period:** 2020-present

Genomic characterisation of antimicrobial resistance in *E. coli* and Gram-negative bacteria from camel production systems in Northern Kenya. Whole genome sequencing of beta-lactamase-producing isolates.

**Collaborators:** Institute of Primate Research, KALRO, George Mason University
